⚙️ How This Works

Your answers are converted into Triangular Fuzzy Numbers (TFNs) using a standard 1–9 linguistic scale. The system then applies Fuzzy TOPSIS (Technique for Order of Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution) to compute a Closeness Coefficient (CC) representing your proximity to the ideal blockchain-ready business profile. Expert criterion weights were pre-calibrated using Fuzzy AHP based on the TOE (Technology-Organisation-Environment) framework. The AI layer uses GPT-4o-mini to translate the mathematical output into plain-language recommendations.
